Black Rhinoceros

Although fierce looking, the rhino is an herbivore that feeds on plants and bushes.

  • Scientific Name: Diceros bicornis
  • Nickname: Hook-Lipped Rhino
  • Habitat: South Africa
  • Conservation Status: Critically Endangered

While named black, this rhino's colors actually vary from brown to gray. They use their horns primarily to defend themselves and are known to be very aggressive against any perceived threat despite having no natural predators. About 50% of Black Rhinos die from combat-related injuries.
